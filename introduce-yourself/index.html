About Sophia

Sophia Cook is a student at McHenry County College who is pursuing a certificate in Web Development. She has a variety of interests and hobbies ranging from theatre to camping. To highlight a few

  • Singing
  • Historical Reenactment
  • Boardgames

These favorites often overlap to form unique experiences such as performing songs about events that happen in her historical reenactment group. Sophia values community deeply and aims to foster it wherever she goes. This sense of community has had an impact on all her interests. Whether it is in a historical reenactment group, a choir, or her own family, she strives to create a place where people can gather and support each other. Being a member of the LGBTQ+ community, Sophia understands the importance of supporting one another.
